Day 21 – Digital Privacy Dashboard

Overview

Built an interactive Digital Privacy Dashboard using AI-assisted development to analyze digital exposure, privacy risks, and data concentration across commonly used online platforms.

What I Learned

- How digital services collect and connect user data across platforms.
- The impact of ecosystem concentration on privacy.
- The role of data visualization in simplifying complex privacy concepts.
- The importance of distinguishing verified facts from estimated insights.

Key Features Explored

- Digital Footprint Score
- Privacy Score
- Exposure Heatmap
- Company Exposure Ranking
- Digital Twin Profile
- Risk Radar
- Privacy Improvement Recommendations

Key Takeaways

- Privacy is not only about data collection but also about how data is linked across services.
- A small number of platforms can gain significant visibility into user behavior.
- Small privacy-focused changes can reduce overall exposure.
- Transparency is essential when presenting privacy-related insights.

Skills Practiced

- AI-Assisted Development
- HTML Dashboard Creation
- Data Visualization
- Privacy Analytics
- Dashboard Testing

Reflection

This project provided valuable insight into digital privacy and demonstrated how dashboards can be used to make data-driven privacy awareness more accessible and understandable.
