

# Day 13 – AI Job Search Agent

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Job Search Agent using Claude AI to discover and analyze relevant job opportunities.

## What I Did

* Created a professional profile using my resume.
* Defined job search criteria for Data Analyst roles.
* Used Claude AI to discover matching job opportunities.
* Reviewed match scores, skill-gap analysis, and market demand insights.
* Documented the results and observations.

## Key Learnings

* AI can automate and personalize the job search process.
* SQL, Python, and Power BI are among the most in-demand skills for Data Analyst roles.
* Building more dashboard projects and improving advanced Excel and statistics skills can increase job readiness.
* Skill-gap analysis helps identify areas for focused improvement.

## Outcome

Successfully generated a list of relevant job opportunities, analyzed market requirements, and gained insights into the skills needed for Data Analyst positions.
