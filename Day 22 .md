
Day 22 – Startup Validation with AI

What I Did

Created and analyzed a Startup Validation Report using AI.

Explored founder-market fit, market sizing, customer personas, competitor analysis, and risk assessment.

Reviewed a complete Go/No-Go recommendation for the startup idea.


Key Learnings

Paying users are the strongest proof that a problem is worth solving.

Market size analysis helps estimate future growth opportunities.

Finding gaps in existing solutions is essential for building a competitive product.

Understanding risks early can save significant time and resources later.

Customer retention is often more important than rapid user acquisition.


Takeaway

This exercise helped me understand how founders and investors evaluate startup ideas before investing time, effort, and money into building them. It was a great introduction to startup thinking and market validation. 🚀
