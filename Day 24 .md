
Day 24 – Business Strategy Report for CreatorLens AI

Today, I focused on the business side of startup building by creating a complete Business Strategy Report for CreatorLens AI.

The report covered key areas such as:

Business Reality Check

Business Model Canvas

Revenue & Pricing Strategy

Go-To-Market Plan

Customer Acquisition Strategy

First 100 Users Roadmap

Competitive Positioning & Moat

Reverse SWOT Analysis

Investment Scorecard


A major takeaway from this exercise was understanding that building a successful startup requires much more than a great product. Customer validation, clear positioning, sustainable revenue models, and effective acquisition strategies are equally important for long-term growth.

This project helped me think like a founder and product manager by connecting customer insights with business execution.
