# Day 16 – Stock Fundamental Research Skill

## Overview
Today I created a reusable Custom Skill in Claude called **stock-fundamental-research**.

The skill generates structured stock research reports using fundamental analysis concepts such as valuation, profitability, growth, financial health, ownership trends, risks, and peer comparisons.

## What I Did
- Created a Custom Skill in Claude.
- Added detailed instructions for stock fundamental analysis.
- Tested the skill on market data examples.
- Generated stock research reports and visual dashboards.
- Documented outputs and observations.

## Key Learnings
- Custom Skills eliminate the need to repeat long prompts.
- Well-structured instructions improve output consistency.
- AI can help organize financial information into a clear format.
- Data sources and verification remain critical for analysis.

## Observations
- The skill produced detailed and structured reports.
- Outputs included valuation metrics, growth indicators, and risk factors.
- Reusable AI workflows can significantly improve productivity.
