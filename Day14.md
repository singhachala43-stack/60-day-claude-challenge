Day 14 – Job Red Flag Detector

Today I used AI to analyze a job opportunity and identify potential risks before applying.

What I Did:
 • Collected a Job Description and company information.
 • Used a Job Red Flag Detector prompt in Claude AI.
 • Generated a risk analysis report.
 • Reviewed red flags, positive signals, and interview questions.

What I Learned:
 • Job descriptions often contain hidden warning signs.
 • Missing information can be a red flag.
 • AI can help evaluate opportunities more efficiently.
 • Asking thoughtful questions during interviews is important.

Tools Used: 
 • Claude AI
 • Job Red Flag Detector Prompt

A small but useful exercise that showed how AI can support smarter career decisions. 🚀
