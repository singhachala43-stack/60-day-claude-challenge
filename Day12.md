# Day 12 - Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit

## Overview

Today, I used AI to build a complete Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit tailored to my career goals as a Data Analyst Intern. By providing my resume and target role, I generated multiple professional resources that can support my internship and job search journey.

## What I Generated

* ATS-Friendly Cover Letter
* Recruiter Outreach Email
* Hiring Manager Email
* LinkedIn Connection Request
* Referral Request Message
* Follow-Up Email
* 30-Second Professional Introduction
* Personal Branding Statement
* Interview Talking Points
* Skill Gap Analysis and Learning Roadmap

## Key Learnings

* AI can help create personalized job search materials much faster.
* Providing detailed context leads to more relevant and useful outputs.
* I identified important skills to improve, including Tableau/Power BI, Advanced SQL, A/B Testing, APIs, and Cloud Basics.
* Personal branding plays a significant role in standing out during job applications.

## Observations

* The generated content was tailored to my background in Data Science, Python, SQL, and Machine Learning.
* The skill gap analysis highlighted areas that are commonly required in Data Analyst roles.
* The learning roadmap provided a clear direction for the next few months.
